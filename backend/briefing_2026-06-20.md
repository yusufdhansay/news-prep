## MFin Buzzwords to Know: 
- **Anarchic World System**: Refers to a global political environment characterized by the absence of a central authority, leading to unpredictable interactions among nations.
- **Supervisory Data Quality Score**: A metric used to assess the accuracy, completeness, and reliability of data reported by financial institutions to regulatory bodies.
- **Provisioning**: The process by which banks set aside funds to cover potential losses on loans and other assets, reflecting their assessment of credit risk.
- **Democratization of Services**: The expansion of access to public services, such as passport delivery, to a wider segment of the population, aiming to reduce inequality and enhance citizen convenience.
